What is Bright.AI?

Bright is a website that Predicts the type of Skin Disease and provides the result as a possibility. It acts as a one stop solution where people can find the result without waiting in long queue for the result and it also has extended features where you can interact with doctors without any delay and can find solutions to be cured before any fatality happens.

Analyze Your Skin Disease with the Power of AI

  • Step 1 Select the type of disease whose possibility is to be checked.
  • Step 2 Upload the image of the affected area as an input.
  • Step 3 Bright AI Detects the skin in the image and create a Region of Interest (ROI).
  • Step 4 It Detects the skin from ROI and feed it to the classifier..
  • Step 5 Classifier will categorize whether skin has a disease or not and provide solutions.
